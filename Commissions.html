Terms and Conditions

How to order

Submit to the google form linked on the left, or send an email to wyzktch.art@gmail.com

Order Details

After you submit your commission request, price will be discussed privately through Gmail. Each commission should take about a week or two to make, but it may take longer depending on how busy I am with school or other things, and I'm comfortable with refunds if it takes too long. Commissions are 1st come 1st serve, and deadlines will have to be discussed privately. If there's something you'd like me to change about the commission, it may or may not cost you extra depending on the difficulty to change it. I only accept PayPal, and its up to you if you want to pay before I send the commission or not, but if you pay after you will receive a watermarked version until payment.

Commercial use

I am ok with commercial commissions, but only under the condition you credit me and pay a $10 fee.
